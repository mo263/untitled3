About The Middle Man

Hello my name is Michelle Osei-Tutu. After my first semester in college I realized college can be very expensive, and am not speaking of tuition. TextBooks,laptops, cellphones, NoteBooks, online access codes etc. It is draining to be a full/part time student and think about money for books. Each semester students spend about 500-1000 dollars on text-books and access codes. and focus on getting good grade. Because of this i created the Middle Man. The main purpose of the Middle Man is to help make the lives of students easier financially. Not only are you able to sell products, students can also donate and tutor each other online. The Middle Man is a non-profit organization, and is operated by one person. We hope in the next year or two it expands and fulfill the needs of all students. The Middle Man was founded in 2019
